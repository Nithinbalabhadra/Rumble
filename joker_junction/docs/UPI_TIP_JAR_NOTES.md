# UPI Tip Jar (Interim Monetization)

Yes, using your personal UPI QR for voluntary tips is technically possible for now.

## Suggested in-app wording
- "Joker Junction is free to play. Tips are optional and non-refundable donations."
- "Tips do not affect game outcomes."

## Product constraints
- Keep gameplay fully free and fair.
- Do not tie winnings, ranking boosts, or match advantage to tips.
- Keep a separate Premium entitlement flow (server-verified) for any paid cosmetic features.

## Recommended next step
When scale grows, migrate from personal UPI to a formal business payment setup and compliance-reviewed receipts.

## Joker Junction Host Pass (₹101/week)
Once you transition to formal payments (Razorpay), the Host Pass model replaces UPI tips:
- Guests always play FREE
- Only hosts pay ₹101/week to create rooms
- Paywall is automated, no manual verification needed after integration
